Digital Tools

Peabody Picture Vocabulary Test (PPTV)

Description
Peabody Picture Vocabulary Test (PPTV) is a standardized assessment tool used to evaluate receptive vocabulary skills. The children are simply presented with pictures along with a verbal label presented by the SLP.
Target Area

Receptive Vocabulary Skills

Duration
Approx. 15-20 minutes
Procedure
The children is presented with 4 pictures. The task is to measure their understanding of vocabulary. With Lipmic, the childen can point out the pictures using their articulation of labels.
