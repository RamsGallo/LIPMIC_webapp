Important! The duration of an assessment digital testing varies according to age, history of previous assessments, the severity of the speech/language deficit, as well as the collaboration and attention of the person.

During the initial parent or client interview, client/parent, physician, and teacher concerns will be discussed. Additionally, will examine medical history, developmental milestones, language acquisition (bilingual clients), educational history and family history.

During the informal assessment portion, use a variety of toys, books or videos to elicit a spontaneous speech and language sample. Then transcribe and analyze the spontaneous sample to determine if there are any speech and/or language anomalies. For instance, analyze a child’s language sample to determine if their language consists of all the age-appropriate morphemes. Also take a narrative language sample to informally analyze higher level language skills (e.g. sequencing, reasoning, perspective taking) according to age.

A digital assessment can be conducted for speech, language, voice, fluency and swallowing using digital tools.

Digital Tools

Goldman Fristoe Articulation Test – 3rd edition (GFTA-3)

Description
The Goldman-Fristoe Test of Articulation 3 (GFTA-3) is a standardized assessment tool used to evaluate articulation skills. This test analyzes articulation only in the English language.
Target Area

Articulation

Duration
Approx. 15-20 minutes
Administered Age
Two to Twenty-one (2-21) years old
